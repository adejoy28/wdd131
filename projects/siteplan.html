Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of my Dry Oar White Water Rafting website is to get an online presence thereby exposing us and our business to the social world, even far and wide leading to more business and eventually more profit. To achieve this, the website will be exhibiting what Dry Oar water rafting is all about which entails fun, adventurous and safe activities which relieve stress, improve team or family bonds. Also empahasizing on the saftey measures the company has put in place for our clients and customers.

Audience

The audience for my site will be people with the age of 8yrs and above. This includes individuals and families of group of people that wants a fun, adventurous and exciting time on water.
